*A short introduction to the lesson progress

The six violin textbooks to be introduced from now on are essential to learning in the progress of violin lessons. Regardless of whether you learn to play the violin as a hobby or to become a performer, these six textbooks are a must-have process to play the violin properly. For those who aim to be performers, these six textbooks will become a base and then the lessons will be conducted to teach the specific violin music they want to play.

Textbook Description
Textbook 1: SUZUKI The textbook contains various songs for each level of difficulty, so you can enjoy playing the violin.
Textbook 2: THIRD POSITION It is a textbook that practices the basic position of the violin from 2 to 7 positions.
Textbook 3: HRIMALY It is a textbook that can help you practice the violin scale and learn the correct notes.
Textbook 4: HOHMANN It is a textbook that can help you improve your skills by learning various practice songs from 1 to 7 positions.
Textbook 5: SEVCIK School of Violin Technics It is a textbook for training the left finger pressing the violin fingerboard.
Textbook 6: SEVCIK School of Bowing Technics It is a textbook that allows you to practice various bowing Technics for violin performance.
