Experience of after-school violin teacher


25 years of violin teacher


I am a violin teacher who has been teaching students for 25 years after graduating from university. In the meantime, I've been teaching various students from kindergarten to high school, and I've taught them at their level so that they can enjoy playing the violin. Violin is a musical instrument that you have to learn well from the basics. If you build your skills step by step from the basics, you'll be a competent student who can enjoy playing the violin.
