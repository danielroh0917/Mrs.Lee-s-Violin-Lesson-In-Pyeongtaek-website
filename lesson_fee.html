*Important note

The lesson fee is clearly shown in the table above. There are only 3 options in lesson time which are 40, 60, and 80 minutes. This is because the nature of the violin requires a lot of concentration and stamina. It is important to keep the right posture when playing the violin. Therefore, unless it's a special case, we don't give lessons for more than 80 minutes.

The maximum number of students per lesson is two at the same time. To be honest, the most efficient lesson is to proceed individually. However, we decided to proceed with up to two people simultaneously for those who prefer group lessons. But 40-minute lessons don't take duo lessons. This is because 40 minutes is an absurdly short time to teach two students at the same time.
